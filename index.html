About Me

Hi, I’m Daisy! I live in Gibraltar with my 3-year-old daughter Harlequin, my husband, and our two dogs. I started my Instagram page (@daisy_in_gibraltar) to document my pregnancy, and it’s grown into a vibrant space where I share day-to-day life, from toddler adventures and parenting moments to travel, food, fashion, and everything in between.

My content is all about real family moments, adventures big and small, and style that works for busy mums. I work with brands that feel like a natural fit for how we live and what we love, creating high-quality UGC that’s relatable, authentic, and built to connect with modern audiences.
